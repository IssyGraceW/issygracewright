Isabelle Grace Wright
Bespoke Fashion Designer | Creative Visionary | Alteration Specialist

Welcome to the world of Isabelle Grace Wright, a rising talent in contemporary fashion design. Combining creativity, precision, and individuality, Isabelle creates bespoke pieces tailored perfectly to each client — ensuring every design is as unique as the person wearing it. From elegant custom garments to expert alterations, Isabelle’s passion for fashion is reflected in every stitch and silhouette. Her work blends modern trends with timeless craftsmanship, producing designs that stand out both on the runway and in everyday style. A graduate of Manchester Metropolitan University, Isabelle has already showcased her talents through fashion catwalk projects and creative design collections, earning recognition as a promising name to watch in the fashion industry. Whether creating one-of-a-kind statement pieces, refining garments through expert tailoring, or bringing a client’s vision to life, Isabelle approaches every project with dedication, artistry, and attention to detail.
